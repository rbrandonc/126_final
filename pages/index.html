Welcome!

The purpose of Carolina Barbell is to help Carolina students to prepare for and compete in strength sport competitions (i.e. weightlifting, powerlifting, strongman).


Carolina Barbell will allow serious strength athletes to form a unified organization that helps each individual realize their goals and potential. The solidarity, support, and shared learning offered by an organized group is critical to individual success. It allows athletes to share goals, tips, assistance, and mostly importantly motivation. Carolina Barbell would provide the stable support, encouragement, and positive mental reinforcement required to endure the grueling training and frequent disappointment of strength sports.


We are applying to become a sports club so our athletes have access to more encompassing university support. The most significant aspect of that support would be funding for meets. Specifically, we will be coordinating and traveling to local, regional, and national meets for the sports of weightlifting, powerlifting, and strongman. The trips to lifting meets will also solidify our desire for improvement, reinvigorate goal setting, and provide team-bonding experiences.


This organization is crucial to the safety of students on campus who are not fully exposed to proper barbell training methods and do not have a grasp on the fundamentals of strength development. Most students exercise by doing cardio and machine-based strength training. While this approach has its respective benefits, students miss the opportunity to commit to a sustainable strength training progression with the use of a barbell. And for many, beginning a program centered around complex and technical lifts on their own, without access to experienced athletes who can provide advice, guidance, and safety tips, can be daunting. Carolina Barbell can help these casual lifters reimagine their gym routine with safe coaching and encouragement.


Besides allowing competitive lifters to attend meets, linking lifters of all types will help even non-competitive lifters succeed with their strength development. Students interested in general strength acquisition will be motivated and assisted by their experiences with peers. Carolina Barbell will make those experiences happen.
