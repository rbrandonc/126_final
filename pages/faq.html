Frequently Asked Questions

Do I have to have previous experience or be an athlete to be a member?

No. The purpose of this club is to teach and encourage the practice of safe strength-training and general health. Whether you're a beginner or an experienced competitor, you're welcome to join.

We regularly host workshops hosted by our officers, members, and guest speakers targeted towards beginners wanting to learn basic to advanced technique.


What is Powerlifting? Weightlifting? Strongman? Crossfit?

All of the above are some of the major competative strength/fitness sports. Below are links to learn more about the differences between each of the sports:

Where is the Tar Heel Training Center (THTC)?

The THTC is located in the Student Recreation Center (208 South Road, Chapel Hill, NC 27599) in Racquetball Ct. #6. This is where most of the workouts, meetings, and seminars will be held.


Do I have to compete?

No. While a major goal of this club is to help students to prepare for and compete in strength sport competitions, you are not required to compete to be a member. You will be encouraged to set goals for progression, but those goals are personal to you.
